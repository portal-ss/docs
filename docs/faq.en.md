# FAQ

## Purchase and Orders

??? question "How do I buy a verification?"
    Pick a platform, region, and verification type in the bot, then confirm the order. The cost is deducted from your internal balance, which you top up in advance with cryptocurrency.

??? question "Which exchanges are supported?"
    Currently — Bybit, Mexc, and Kucoin. The list is being expanded; the current selection is always visible in the bot's catalog.

??? question "The geo I need is not in the catalog — what should I do?"
    If a country is not displayed, there are currently not enough Executors for it. The catalog is updated as new Executors become available.

??? question "How does a wholesale order differ from a standard one?"
    Wholesale orders differ only in their reduced price and are available only with an active subscription.

??? question "What is a team order?"
    A team order is a mode in which the work is performed by Executors of your own Team, invited by you personally. You choose any geo and set the price per slot yourself; the full amount goes to the Executor. Requires an active subscription and at least one active Team member.

??? question "How do I upload an account to the bot?"
    Two methods are available: via **ADS Power** (cookie export as a .txt file) and **manual** (you extract the cookies of each account using an extension). A detailed guide is shown in the bot during upload.


## Payment and Balance

??? question "How do I top up the balance?"
    Get your personal deposit address in the bot and send USDT or USDC to it. Supported networks: Binance Smart Chain (BSC), Polygon, Arbitrum. The credit is applied after the transaction is confirmed on the network.

??? question "What is the minimum top-up amount?"
    1 USDT.

??? question "I sent a deposit on the wrong network or in the wrong token — what should I do?"
    Unfortunately, such funds are usually **impossible to recover**. Before sending, always verify the network, token, and address. If the mistake has already occurred — contact support.

??? question "The deposit didn't arrive — what should I do?"
    Contact support and send the transaction hash. We will check at which stage it got stuck.

??? question "What is the referral balance?"
    These are credits you receive for inviting other clients. The referral balance can be spent on all Service services.

??? question "In what order are funds debited when paying?"
    First from the referral balance, then from the main one. Refunds are credited back to the original debit sources proportionally.

## Subscriptions

??? question "What does a subscription give me?"
    Access to wholesale prices and the ability to create team orders. The subscription lasts a fixed number of days.

??? question "What happens to my orders if the subscription expires?"
    Orders bound to the subscription are automatically frozen: no new slots can be uploaded or claimed. Executors who are already working are given a grace period to check the verification status — if during this window the exchange confirms successful verification, the slot is completed properly.

??? question "Can I extend my subscription in advance?"
    Yes. Purchasing a new subscription while the previous one is still active extends its term.

??? question "Is the subscription fee refundable?"
    No, the subscription purchase **is not refundable**.

## Verification

??? question "How long does verification take?"
    It depends on the availability of Executors in the required geo and on the processing speed of the exchange. There are no fixed timeframes — usually from tens of minutes to a few hours.

??? question "Can I speed it up?"
    No. All orders go into a common pool and are picked up by Executors based on their availability; the Service does not provide priority processing for individual slots.

??? question "How do I know that the verification has passed?"
    The bot will send a notification when the status changes. The status is also always visible in the slot card.

??? question "The exchange rejected the verification with no option to retry — what next?"
    With a "BLOCKED" status, funds for the slot are returned automatically. The order can be created again using a different account.

## Re-verification

??? question "Do you guarantee the completion of re-verification?"
    Executors are notified in advance of the possible need to perform re-verification on the slot they completed; however, the actual performance depends solely on the willingness and availability of the Executor.

??? question "How much does re-verification cost?"
    For standard and wholesale slots the catalog price of the corresponding type applies; for team slots — the price you set yourself. The exact amount is shown before confirming the order.

??? question "Can I cancel a re-verification?"
    Yes — within an established window after order creation.

## Account Security

??? question "Does the Executor get access to my account?"
    No. Executors do not gain access to uploaded sessions and perform only the operations provided by the Service's interface (initiating verification and checking its status).

??? question "Won't this look suspicious to the exchange?"
    The work is performed within your own active session that you provided to the Service — without a separate login to the account from other devices.

## Refunds

??? question "In what cases is a refund possible?"
    Before the slot is taken into work — a full refund on request. After — if the exchange has closed the possibility of further verification attempts on the account, as well as in case of Executor inactivity beyond the established period.

??? question "Where do refunded funds go?"
    To the internal balance, to the same debit sources from which the payment was made (proportionally between the main and referral balances).

??? question "Can I withdraw money from the balance back out?"
    The cash balance is intended to pay for the Service's services. For questions about withdrawing funds outside the platform, contact support.
